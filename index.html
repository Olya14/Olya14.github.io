Why she is a perfect leader

Izumi is one of the best leaders in anime I have seen so far. She is mature, composed and fearless. While everyone in the class only cared about being alive, she genuienly tried to protect everyone. She, also is more mature than any of the teachers shown in the series, might you, and makes the decisions that any teacher would be scared to counsider. Also, I'm shocked that at the age of fifteen, she managed to take that high stess dead-end position. I think she understood, that no one in the class is caprable with dealing with that kind of stress.
